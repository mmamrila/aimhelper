DPI (Dots Per Inch)

Hardware sensitivity of your mouse sensor. Higher DPI means more cursor movement per inch of physical movement.

Pixel Skipping

When sensitivity is too low, crosshairs "jump" over pixels instead of smooth transitions, affecting precision.

Prevention: Maintain reasonable DPI (400+ recommended)
