🎯 The Science Behind the Platform

AimHelper Pro uses a multi-test analysis approach to determine your optimal sensitivity settings based on your natural aim patterns and performance data.

🧠

Performance Analysis

Our system analyzes your accuracy, reaction time, and consistency across different test scenarios to identify your optimal sensitivity range.

⚡ The 4-Test Analysis Process

Each test evaluates different aspects of your aiming ability to build a complete performance profile

🔄

Circle Tracking Test

What it measures: Smooth tracking ability and micro-adjustments

How it works:

  • Follow a moving circular target for 30 seconds
  • Measures how closely you can maintain cursor position on the target
  • Evaluates smooth mouse movements and hand stability
  • Tests your ability to make fine adjustments while tracking

What this tells us: Whether you perform better with higher or lower sensitivities for sustained tracking movements.

🎯

Grid Shot Test

What it measures: Precision clicking and target acquisition

How it works:

  • Click targets that appear randomly on a grid
  • Measures accuracy percentage and reaction times
  • Tests point-and-click precision under time pressure
  • Evaluates your ability to quickly acquire stationary targets

What this tells us: Your optimal sensitivity for precise aiming and quick target acquisition.

⚡

Flick Test

What it measures: Quick target-to-target movements

How it works:

  • Quickly move between targets that appear at different distances
  • Measures speed and accuracy of large mouse movements
  • Tests your ability to make fast directional changes
  • Evaluates flick accuracy across various distances

What this tells us: Whether you excel with faster sensitivities for quick flicks or prefer slower settings for accuracy.

📊

Consistency Test

What it measures: Muscle memory and repeatability

How it works:

  • Repeat the same movement pattern multiple times
  • Measures how consistently you can reproduce movements
  • Tests muscle memory and movement precision
  • Evaluates your natural sensitivity preference through repetition

What this tells us: The sensitivity range where your muscle memory performs most consistently.

🔬 How We Calculate Your Optimal Settings

Our algorithm combines your test results using game-specific weighting to find your ideal sensitivity

1

Performance Scoring

Each test result receives a combined performance score based on accuracy, reaction time, and consistency metrics.

2

Game-Specific Weighting

Results are weighted based on your chosen game's requirements. For example, tactical shooters like Valorant emphasize accuracy, while fast-paced games like Apex Legends prioritize reaction time.

3

Top Performance Analysis

We identify your best-performing test sessions and analyze the sensitivity settings used during those sessions.

4

Optimal Calculation

Using your preferred DPI, we calculate the in-game sensitivity that produces the same in/360° as your best-performing tests.

⚠️ Important Information

Understanding the limitations and best practices for sensitivity optimization

💡

Key Points to Remember:

  • Individual Results: Optimal sensitivity varies greatly between players based on physical factors, experience, and personal preference
  • Practice Required: Any sensitivity change requires practice time to develop muscle memory
  • Game-Specific: You may need different sensitivities for different games or roles within the same game
  • Hardware Matters: Mouse sensor quality, mousepad surface, and grip style all affect optimal settings
  • Gradual Changes: Large sensitivity changes should be made gradually to avoid disrupting existing muscle memory
